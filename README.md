## Project Objective
This project analyses employee performance, productivity and HR metrics using Excel. The goal is to help HR managers and business leaders make data-driven decisions by identifying trends in employee performance, satisfaction and productivity.

## Final Conclusion
- The Employee Performance & Productivity Analysis Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of workforce performance, productivity, and employee engagement. By analyzing key HR metrics such as performance scores, training hours, work hours, overtime, projects handled, satisfaction, and retention, the dashboard helps identify trends that support better business decisions.
- The analysis shows that employee performance is influenced by factors such as training, workload, and departmental differences. It also highlights productivity patterns across departments, enabling management to identify high-performing teams and areas that may require additional support or resources.
- Overall, this project demonstrates how Excel can transform raw HR data into meaningful insights through interactive dashboards. The dashboards help HR managers and business leaders monitor employee performance, optimize workforce productivity, improve employee satisfaction, and make data-driven decisions that contribute to organizational success.
